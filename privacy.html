Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with the AstraNova Service, we collect the following information:

  • Account Data: Your chosen Commander Identifier (username) and a securely hashed version of your Security Key (password). We do not store plain-text passwords.
  • Fleet Data: Digital records of the ships, stats, and configurations you manually save to your Personal Dock.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to maintain your personal session, calculate your fleet technology points, and save your progress. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Local Storage (Cookies)

AstraNova uses browser LocalStorage strictly to store your secure authentication token (allowing you to stay logged in) and to remember your local resource counts (Oil, Coins, Cubes). We do not use tracking cookies.

4. Third-Party Payment Processing

If you choose to support the archive, payments are processed securely by Stripe. AstraNova does not collect, process, or store your credit card information. Please refer to Stripe's Privacy Policy for more details regarding payment data.

5. Data Deletion

You may request the complete deletion of your Commander account and all associated fleet data by contacting commander@astranova.com.
